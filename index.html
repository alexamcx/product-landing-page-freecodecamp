ceremonial grade

The highest quality matcha, meticulously hand-picked from the youngest tea leaves of the Camellia Sinensis plant. It has a vibrant green color, smooth texture, and a well-balanced flavor profile with subtle sweetness and umami.

✪

premium grade

Offers a slightly less vibrant green color and a milder flavor compared to ceremonial grade, but it is still suitable for traditional tea preparation. It can also be used in matcha lattes, smoothies, and baked goods.

✤

culinary grade

Made from more mature tea leaves, which results in a slightly bitter taste and a less vibrant green color. Provides a pleasant green tea flavor when mixed with other ingredients, making it ideal for matcha-flavored desserts, ice creams, and sauces.
